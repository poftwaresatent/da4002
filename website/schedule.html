ITADS Schedule

Official Schedule

The TimeEdit schedule is authoritative, so if there are differences between that and the list below, TimeEdit contains the correct information.

Lectures

Lectures usually take place on Tuesday mornings 8h15-10h in room D415. But sometimes the room, date, or time has to change!

Exercises

Exercises take place in UNIX room B231c on various days and at various times (two sessions per week total). The guided exercises focus on providing additional details about the topics covered in the lectures and allow the students to practice what they learn. Depending on the number of students the class can be divided into two exercise groups (A and B), and each group gets two exercise sessions per week, but students who need more time can come to more than two sessions if there is enough space.

Projects

Two projects are performed in teams of two students and are required in addition to the exam for passing the course. Projects require more self-guidance than the exercises, but the lecturer is available for help during the scheduled hours in the UNIX room B231c. The teams have to hand in their source code and write a short report about each project.
